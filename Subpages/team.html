Svenja Guhr

Svenja is a research associate in the fortext lab and has an academic background in German literature, Romance linguistics, and Digital Humanities. As a computational literary studies scholar, her PhD project focuses on the operationalisation and analysis of sound and loudness as narratological phenomena in German-language literary prose of the 19th and early 20th century. She is a guest researcher at the Göttingen Centre for Digital Humanities and at the Stanford Literary Lab.
